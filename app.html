TravelBug

Your travel. Automatically tracked and visualized.
TravelBug turns your flights, trips, and locations into a personal travel dashboard. Track your travel history, visualize your journey on a world map, and unlock insights like miles flown, countries visited, and travel patterns.
• Import flights from your calendar
• Track countries, cities, and trips
• View travel stats and insights
• Visualize routes on an interactive map
• Add notes and memories to trips
View on the App Store
TravelBug reads calendar data only with your permission to identify travel-related events. All data remains on your device and is never sold or shared.
