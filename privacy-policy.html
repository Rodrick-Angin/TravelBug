TravelBug - Around The World Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

TravelBug - Around The World respects your privacy.

TravelBug does not collect, store, or transmit personal information to external servers.

All travel data such as places visited, flights, routes, notes, and statistics are processed and stored locally on your device and/or in your private iCloud storage (if enabled).

Google Calendar Data Access

If you choose to grant permission, TravelBug may access your Google Calendar data in a read-only manner to identify travel-related events such as flights.

This may include limited event information such as:

  • Event titles
  • Dates and times
  • Locations

This data is used solely to detect and organize your travel activity within the app.

TravelBug does not modify your calendar, and access is strictly read-only.

All processing of calendar data occurs locally on your device. No Google user data is transmitted to external servers, stored remotely, or shared with third parties.

Google user data is used only to provide core app functionality and is not used for advertising, profiling, or resale.

Calendar Access (Device)

If you choose to grant permission, TravelBug may also read calendar data from your device to identify and import travel-related events. This data remains on your device and is never uploaded or shared.

Data Sharing

TravelBug does not sell, share, or transmit your data to third parties.

User Control

You can revoke permissions (such as calendar access) at any time through your device settings or your Google account permissions.

If you choose to share travel information using iOS sharing features, that information is shared only with the services you select.

Third-Party Services

Map functionality uses Apple Maps and may be subject to Apple’s privacy policies.
